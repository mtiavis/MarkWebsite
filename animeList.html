Kino's Journey (NOT THE 2017 version):

So the main character is named a young boy named Kino who has a talking motorcycle named Hermes. No this is not weird and the motorcycle is not annoying. They go to different cities that have different customs and traditions that are fun to watch about. There's a story about a city who puts all their faith in a church which leads to them believing that the end of the world is near and another story of a city where everyone can read each others thoughts. It makes the viewer wonder "what would happen if a city like this did exist".

Kubikiri Cycle:

If you ever read an Agatha Christie book, then this show has that feeling to it. There is an island in the middle of nowhere that houses all sorts of "geniuses". There's a genius in art, cooking, programming, and much more. However, a murder occurs one night and now it is up for the characters to stay alive and capture the murderer. I personally liked the "impossible murder scenarios" that makes you really stump on how a murder was even achieved.
